# Senior Full-Stack Engineer — Stripe

**Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Team:** Developer Platform

## About the Role

We're looking for a Senior Full-Stack Engineer to join the Developer Platform team at Stripe. You'll work on the tools, APIs, and infrastructure that millions of developers use to build their businesses.

## What You'll Do

- Design, build, and maintain APIs and services that power Stripe's developer experience
- Collaborate with product, design, and engineering teams to ship features that simplify complex financial workflows
- Improve the reliability, performance, and scalability of our developer-facing products
- Write clean, well-tested code and participate in thorough code reviews
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to engineering culture

## What We're Looking For

- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript, React, and Node.js (or similar stack)
- Experience designing and building RESTful or GraphQL APIs
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or equivalent)
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- Track record of shipping products end-to-end, from ideation to production
- Strong communication skills and a collaborative mindset

## Nice to Have

- Experience with payment systems or financial infrastructure
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience building developer tools, SDKs, or CLI tooling
- Familiarity with distributed systems concepts

## Why Stripe

- Competitive salary and equity
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage
- Flexible hybrid work model
- Learning & development budget
- Work on products used by millions of businesses worldwide
